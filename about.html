Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate technologists eager to share ideas on innovation, software, and hardware development. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the world’s premier technology conferences, attracting experts, startups, and industry leaders.

From humble beginnings in a single hall to now filling major convention centers, TechCon has been a driving force behind collaboration and inspiration in the tech community.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon 2024 is to empower innovators, entrepreneurs, and thinkers to shape the future of technology responsibly and inclusively.

We aim to foster collaboration, share emerging technologies, and promote solutions that address global challenges through technology.
